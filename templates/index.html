Welcome to the Pyramid Poker

Pyramid Poker is a new variation of poker invented by Philip Lee and Peter Yee.

Pyramid Poker has many variations. While the game can be adjusted for different numbers of players, these are the instructions for the Solitaire Version the game. The game requires 3 decks of cards plus 3 jokers to play (159 cards). A joker is a wild card and can be played as any card you wish. All 159 cards are shuffled to start the play.

You are initially dealt 20 cards. If you choose to play, 5 more cards will be dealt to you per the following game instructions.

The goal is to arrange at least 20 cards into 6 distinct hands

  • 1 one-card hand,
  • 3 three card hands,
  • and 2 five-card hands

    • Each player will compete with his/her 6 hands.

    Hand 6 must be stronger than Hand 5, which must be stronger than Hand 4, and so on and so forth. The value of each hand follows traditional poker rules (e.g., a flush beats a straight).
